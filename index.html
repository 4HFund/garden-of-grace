Garden of Grace

First Harvest

Restore an overgrown field through faith, peace, kindness, and patient cultivation.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow pad to move.
  • Select a seed type from the seed tray.
  • Stand on a soil tile to plant.
  • Water planted seeds to help them grow.
  • Clear weeds to restore the field.
  • Pray once each day for a spiritual growth boost.
  • Harvest mature crops to increase your stats.
